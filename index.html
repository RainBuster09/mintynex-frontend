🛡️ Trust & Safety

Built for Safe Trading

Every feature on MintyNex is designed to protect trainers and keep the community honest.

  • ✅
    Verified Trainer Badges
    Apply for verification after 5+ completed trades and ID check. Verified trainers get a badge visible on all listings.
  • ⭐
    Star Rating System
    Every trade leaves a rating. Build your reputation over time — bad actors get flagged fast.
  • 🚩
    Report & Dispute System
    Flag suspicious listings or file a dispute. Our admin team reviews every case within 24 hours.
  • 🔒
    In-Person Only Trades
    No money moves through MintyNex. All trades are done face to face — eliminating shipping scams entirely.
